bingus.link Privacy Policy

This policy extends upon the policy featured at https://trevorthalacker.com/legal/privacy.

When you create a short URL, we do not collect any information about the Short URL in any form. The destination URL is encrypted, and is only decrypted upon trying to visit the short URL page.
We cannot view the short URL destination page, and there are no analytics, nor any other forms of tracking on short URL pages.

Short URL destination pages are not controlled by us, and may be subject to their own policies.

All information in our database is completely anonymous, and we cannot determine who created the Short URL, nor any other information. The only information we have is the Date and Time of creation.

If you have any questions about this policy, please do not hesitate to contact us at privacy@trevorthalacker.com.

If you do not agree to any part of this policy, the only action you can take would be to cease use of this website.
